ESG Analysis Overview

Environmental Impact: The Environmental Score evaluates a company’s commitment to preserving natural resources, managing waste, reducing emissions, and utilizing renewable energy.

Social Responsibility: The Social Score highlights a company’s impact on its employees, customers, and communities, measuring factors like employee satisfaction, health and safety, and workforce diversity.

Governance Policies: The Governance Score focuses on ethical business practices, transparency, and strong leadership.
